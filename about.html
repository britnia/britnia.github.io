I'm a self-taught software engineer, former soldier, diversity enthusiast and bike life afficianado based in Atlanta, GA. I'm currently a member of Manheim's IFS Inventory API team with whom I write and maintain Ruby APIs in support of automotive auctions worldwide.

I'm an advocate for junior developers and believe in the power of community to bridge the CS gap. To that end I serve as Event Coordinator on the Rails Girls Atlanta Board and speak frequently at meetups, student organizations and other events to which I am invited. I will make my international conference speaking debut at Brighton Ruby in Brighton, England in July 2016.
